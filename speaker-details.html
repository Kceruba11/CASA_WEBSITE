About Dr. Nnadede

Dr. Nnadede Kasarachi is an independent researcher and academic contributor whose work spans environmental management, safety, and community development. He has authored research on safety management systems in construction companies, identifying critical predictors such as leadership, hazard assessment, and communication.

With a strong interest in sustainable development, Dr. Kasarachi’s research also explores the integration of biodiversity into environmental impact assessments — bringing together ecological preservation and responsible development

His expertise makes him a powerful voice at the Akwete Summit, where he will speak on themes around smart connectivity and local empowerment, bridging the gap between culture, innovation, and community growth.
